ABOUT US


Tough times such as the coronavirus pandemic calls for responsible communication. However, a majority of the people facing issues due to the lockdown, are neither able to communicate their problems nor are able to reach out to other authorities. We understand that most of the people are getting assisted, but there are still some areas that need help and we aim to reach and speak for them.





CovidHelper is a real-time stats dashboard where you can fill out the problems you are facing in your area/locality and can also track the neighbouring areas who might need your help with basic amenities. CovidHelper will provide your with authentic data and information about Coronovirus updates in our country. This information will be shared with NGO's and other higher authorities and they can monitor the areas close to them.




CovidHelper is developed and managed by Unicorn Gaming Hub which is an interactive technology (AR/VR/MR) based startup in Gurugram, Haryana. Equipped with a global outlook, we are a young and dynamic team of creative individuals focused on cultivating a unique and enriching user experience in the fields of AR/VR, mixed reality and interactive gaming.




At U.G.H, we believe that games have the power to awaken and enlighten the masses. Our recipe for this is simple: preparation and insight. Ready to explore beyond the boundaries of existing technologies.
